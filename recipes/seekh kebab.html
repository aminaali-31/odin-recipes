Seekh kebab

Home

Description

Seekh kebab is a popular South Asian dish made from spiced minced meat shaped around skewers and grilled or pan-fried. It is juicy, smoky, and full of flavor, often served with naan, chutney, and salad.

Ingredients

  • 500 g minced chicken or beef
  • 1 small onion (very finely chopped)
  • 2 tbsp ginger–garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies (finely chopped)
  • 2 tbsp fresh coriander
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp breadcrumbs or roasted gram flour
  • Sllt to taste
  • Oil or butter for cooking

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix mince, onion, ginger–garlic, chilies, coriander, spices, egg, and breadcrumbs.
  2. Mix well until sticky. Rest 15 minutes.
  3. Shape mixture onto skewers or make long kebab shapes by hand
  4. Heat grill pan or frying pan with little oil.
  5. Cook kebabs on medium heat, turning until golden and fully cooked
  6. Serve hot with naan, chutney, and salad.
