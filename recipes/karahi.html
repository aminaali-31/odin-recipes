Karahi

Home

Description

Karahi is a famous Pakistani dish cooked in a wok-like pan called a “karahi.” It has a rich tomato-based gravy with bold spices and is best eaten fresh with naan or roti.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Garlic Ginger paste
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Chilli
  • Water

Steps

  1. Heat oil in karahi. Add chicken and fry until lightly golden.
  2. Add ginger–garlic paste, cook 1 minute.
  3. Add tomatoes, salt, chili, turmeric, and coriander seeds.
  4. Cook on high heat until oil separates.
  5. Add green chilies and black pepper
  6. Cook 5 more minutes on high, stirring.
  7. Garnish with ginger and coriander. Serve hot with naan.
